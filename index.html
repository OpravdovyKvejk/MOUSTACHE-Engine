What is it?

MOUSTACHE Engine is a free-to-use 3D engine currently in development under TSOYM Games. It is being written for ZETH, an upcoming oldschool shooter inspired by such classics as DOOM and Quake (both DOOM and Quake are trademarks owned by id Software)

When is it getting released?

The estimated release date of ZETH is 17th May 2018. MOUSTACHE Engine will be released a week after that.

The team

TSOYM Games is an independant company based in Ostrava, Czech Republic. ZETH and MOUSTACHE Engine are their first major projects.

Contributors

The MOUSTACHE Engine would not exist without the help of the very talented Matěj Štágl. His contributions include shaders (SSAO, texture mapping, ...).
